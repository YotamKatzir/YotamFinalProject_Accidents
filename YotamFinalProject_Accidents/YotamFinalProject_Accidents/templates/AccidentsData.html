USA accidents data page

Field Description
ID This is a unique identifier of the accident record.
Source Indicates source of the accident report.
TMC A Traffic Message Channel code which provides more detailed description of the event.
Severity Shows the severity of the accident in a range of 1 to 4
Start_Time Shows start time of the accident in local time zone.
End_Time Shows end time of the accident in local time zone.
Start_Lat Shows latitude in GPS coordinate of the start point.
Start_Lng Shows longitude in GPS coordinate of the start point.
End_Lat Shows latitude in GPS coordinate of the end point.
End_Lng Shows longitude in GPS coordinate of the end point.
Distance(mi) The length of the road extent affected by the accident.
Description Shows natural language description of the accident.
Number Shows the street number.
Street Shows the street name.
Side Shows the relative side of the street.
City Shows the city.
County Shows the county in.
State Shows the state.
Zipcode Shows the zipcode.
Country Shows the country
Timezone Shows the timezone.
Airport_Code Shows the nearest airport code.
Weather_Timestamp Shows the weather timestamp at the time of the accident.
Temperature(F) Shows the air temperature in fahrenheit at the time of the accident.
Wind_Chill(F) Shows the wind temperature in fahrenheit at the time of the accident.
Humidity(%) Shows the humidity at the time of the accident.
Pressure(in) Shows the barometric pressure at the time of the accident.
Visibility(mi) Shows the visibility at the time of the accident.
Wind_Direction Shows the wind direction at the time of the accident.
Wind_Speed(mph) Shows the wind speed at the time of the accident.
Precipitation(in) Shows the precipitation at the time of the accident.
Weather_Condition Shows natural language description of the weather at the time of the accident.
Amenity Shows whether or not there was amenity.
Bump Shows whether or not there was a bump.
Crossing Shows whether or not there was a crossing.
Give_Way Shows whether or not there was "give way" sign.
Junction Shows whether or not there was a junction.
No_Exit Shows whether or not there was a "no exit" sign.
Railway Shows whether or not there was a railway.
Roundabout Shows whether or not there was a roundabout.
Station Shows whether or not there was bus station.
Stop Shows whether or not there was a "stop" sign.
Traffic_Calming Shows whether or not the traffic was calmed.
Traffic_Signal Shows whether or not there was a traffic signal.
Turning_Loop Shows whether or not there was a turning loop.
Sunrise_Sunset Shows if the sun was visible.
Civil_Twilight Shows the civil twilight.
Nautical_Twilight Shows the nautical twilight.
Astronomical_Twilight Shows the astronomical twilight.
